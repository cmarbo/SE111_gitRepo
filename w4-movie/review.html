Let the Magic Begin.

ABOUT CAST REVIEW

Review |

Cameron Arbo August 12, 2021

IMDB Link: https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0241527/?ref_=tttr_tr_tt

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone gives a wonderful insight into the world of fantasy by utilizing such a strong storyline as well as a exceptional cast. My fondest memory is when my father took me to the movie theaters as a young boy to see this once it came out and I absolutely loved the concept of this wizarding world.

Although there were many changes from the book to the movie, as there always is when books have a movie counterpart, there is a reason why there is such a strong fanbase and following. The movie series truly does give a nonstop captivating performance through the storytelling and imagination.


Trivia

  • Did you know that Alan Rickman was handpicked to play Snape by J.K. Rowling and even received special instructions from her about the character?
  • The child actors and actresses would be working on their actual school work during scenes to make the setting more real.
  • The Hogwarts' motto, "Draco dormiens nunquam titillandus", means "never tickle a sleeping dragon".
  • Rosie O'Donnell and Robin Williams were two of the celebrities who had asked for a role in the movie without pay, in their cases, Hagrid and Molly Weasley. They didn't get these roles, because J.K. Rowling wanted a cast strictly from the British Isles.
